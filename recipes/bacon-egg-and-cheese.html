Bacon Egg and Cheese

Description

A bacon egg and cheese is a quick and delicious breakfast sandwich that is popular in New York.

As the name suggests, it is a pretty simple dish, but is definitely greater than the sum of it's parts.

Ingredients

  • Bagel of your choice
  • Bacon
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Ketchup, and or Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Slice and place bagel into the toaster until toasted to your liking
  2. Put bacon on hot skillet and begin cooking
  3. Once bacon is done cooking, place the bacon on a paper towel to drain, and save the bacon grease from the skillet
  4. In another skillet, place about a tablespoon of the bacon grease in
  5. Crack egg into a bowl and give a brief whisk until the yolk and whites have completely mixed
  6. Place egg into greasted skillet with bacon grease and begin cooking
  7. After egg is done cooking, season to taste with salt and pepper, and place a slice of cheese on top of the egg along with the drained bacon
  8. Once cheese is fully melted, it's time to assemble
  9. Place the bacon egg and cheese from the pan onto the toasted bagel
  10. Optionally: top with condiments of your choice such as ketchup and or hot sauce
  11. Enjoy!
