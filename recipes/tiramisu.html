Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a delicious desert served cold that requires no baking, but don't be fooled, it can be decadent.

Among it's most notable features are the distinct coffee taste, and the layers consisting of a sweet cream and coffee-dipped ladyfingers.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder

Steps

  1. Separate egg yolks from egg whites
  2. Mix egg yolks with sugar till fully combined
  3. Mix egg whites with more sugar until stiff peaks form
  4. Add mascarpone to egg yolk mixture
  5. Add vanilla extract to egg yolk and mascarpone mixture
  6. Add in previously mixed egg whites and sugar to egg yolk mixture
  7. In a baking pan, place down a layer of ladyfingers that have been very briefly dipped into some coffee
  8. Spread down a layer of the cream mixture on top of the ladyfingers
  9. Repeat this layering until all ladyfingers and cream are used
  10. Dust with cocoa powder and let sit in fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight
  11. Enjoy!
